What this surface is for

The goal is not to fake a marketplace. The goal is to make extension trust easier to judge.

A good plugin surface should help a technical reader answer practical questions fast: who owns this, how mature is it, what capability does it expose, and how do I verify it without guesswork.

Discovery should stay useful before it becomes crowded.

That is why the public path starts with stable URLs, explicit trust framing, and machine-readable metadata. Visual density can come later, after the runtime and plugin contracts settle.

Until then, the docs remain the deeper source of truth and the plugin surface stays focused on comparison, trust, and public contract clarity.
